"GreaterCommons lets you collect, share, and discover class materials.
GreaterCommons will help you find great teaching materials including videos,
articles, pictures, handouts, presentations and more."

  • create
    "Create a collection of resources. PowerPoint presentations, handouts, links to online resources such as videos, articles, and useful websites - GreaterCommons lets you easily collect, organize, and share course materials."
  • become
    "Become a center of influence. Allow others to discover your resources then copy them into their collections. You will be able to see how many classes and students have benefited from your resources - you will be influencing the quality of classes around the world.
  • find
    "Find great resources. GreaterCommons is a central repository of course resources. Find the resources that have been used the most, and rated the highest, then copy those resources into your collection and use them in your classes."
